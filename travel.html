About Wanderlust Adventures

Wanderlust Adventures began as a personal travel diary in 2015 and has grown into a community of adventure seekers from around the world. Our mission is to inspire responsible travel to wild places while providing practical advice to help you plan your own adventures.

We believe that adventure travel should be accessible to everyone, regardless of budget or experience level. Our team of writers includes seasoned mountaineers, scuba instructors, and long-term backpackers who share their expertise to help you travel safely and sustainably.

When we're not on the road, we're based in Boulder, Colorado, where we test gear, plan future expeditions, and share stories with fellow travelers.
